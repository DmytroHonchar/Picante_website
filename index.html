Bienvenidos

A taste of Guadalajara in the Baltic Triangle

Picante is a Mexican cantina brought to life by four of Liverpool’s most experienced independent hospitality operators — Danny Murphy (Berry & Rye, Botanical Garden), Kate Meehan (Gin Journey, Paloma Café), Joe Wild (Taco or Don’t), and Sally Jo Whitlow (Jenny’s Bar). Set inside the historic Cains Brewery Village, we’re serving Guadalajara-inspired street food, over 40 tequilas and mezcals, frozen margaritas, and a Latin soundtrack from our sun-soaked Más terrace. Walk in, grab a seat, and stay a while.
